About

Rachel is a registered dietitian specializing in disordered eating. She received her bachelors degree in dietetics at the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga, then completed her dietetic internship through Iowa State University. During her internship, Rachel provided individualized care for patients with eating disorders. It was during this time that she discovered her passion for helping others find food freedom and heal their relationship with food. Rachel is an avid lifelong learner and is working toward her Certified Eating Disorder Registered Dietitian (CEDRD) credential. She promotes an anti-diet, all foods fit, intuitive eating approach to treating patients.
